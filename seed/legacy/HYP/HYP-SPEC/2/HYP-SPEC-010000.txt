**Version:** 1.0 Founder Edition

**Status:** Official

**Classification:** Architecture

---

# Purpose

This document defines the global architecture of the Hypatia Knowledge Platform.

It describes the major architectural layers, their responsibilities, and the direction of data flow.

It is the master architectural reference for all future technical specifications.

---

# 1. Architectural Vision

Hypatia is **not** a monolithic application.

It is a layered scientific knowledge infrastructure.

Each layer performs a single responsibility.

Each higher layer depends only on lower layers.

Lower layers never depend on higher layers.

# 3. Architectural Principle

Hypatia has one center.

Everything revolves around it.

The center is:

**Knowledge Repository**

Not the Website.

Not the AI.

Not the Database.

The Repository.

---

# 4. Primary Architectural Layers

## Layer 0

Historical Sources

Raw archaeological evidence.

Nothing is modified.

---

## Layer 1

Knowledge Repository

The scientific heart of Hypatia.

Contains:

* Lemmas
* Entities
* Stories
* Sources
* Grammar
* Domains
* Meta Objects

---

## Layer 2

Resolver Layer

Responsible for:

Identification

Normalization

Matching

Linking

Relationship Discovery

It never stores knowledge.

---

## Layer 3

Knowledge Graph

Transforms isolated knowledge into an interconnected scientific network.

---

## Layer 4

Presentation Layer

Transforms knowledge into information understandable by humans.

Examples:

Website

Dictionary

Story Viewer

Search

Explorer

---

## Layer 5

Training Layer

Produces machine-learning datasets.

The repository is never trained directly.

Only curated datasets are exported.

---

## Layer 6

Artificial Intelligence

Consumes knowledge.

Never owns knowledge.

---

# 5. Data Flow

Every historical fact follows the same lifecycle.

```text
Source

↓

Normalization

↓

Knowledge Card

↓

Resolver

↓

Relationships

↓

Knowledge Graph

↓

Review

↓

Repository

↓

Training Builder

↓

Language Model

↓

Website

↓

Community Feedback

↓

Review

↓

Repository
```

This cycle never ends.

---

# 6. Repository-Centered Design

Every subsystem communicates with the Repository.

Subsystems never communicate directly with each other unless explicitly defined.

This guarantees:

Consistency

Traceability

Maintainability

Scientific integrity

---

# 7. Architectural Independence

Each subsystem must remain independently replaceable.

Examples:

The Website may change.

The AI model may change.

MongoDB may change.

Python may change.

Flutter may change.

The Repository remains unchanged.

---

# 8. Scientific Pipeline

Hypatia intentionally separates:

Knowledge Creation

Knowledge Validation

Knowledge Storage

Knowledge Presentation

Knowledge Learning

No stage performs another stage's responsibility.

---

# 9. Expansion Strategy

The architecture is civilization-independent.

Future civilizations become new knowledge domains.

No redesign is required.

---

# 10. Long-Term Objective

Hypatia is designed to become:

The scientific source of truth

↓

The educational source

↓

The AI training source

↓

The public exploration platform

All generated from one repository.
