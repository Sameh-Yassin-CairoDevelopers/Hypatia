# Purpose

The Lexical Card represents a single lexical concept within the Hypatia Repository.

It is the central linguistic object around which translation, grammar, occurrences, meanings, and semantic relationships are organized.

Every lexical item is represented by exactly one Lexical Card.

---

---

# Definition

A Lexical Card describes a lexical unit independently from any particular text.

It does not represent one occurrence.

It represents the concept shared by all occurrences.

---

# Universal Structure

The Lexical Card inherits every field defined in

HYP-SPEC-020000

and additionally defines the following sections.

---

# Section 1

## Lexical Identity

```text
lemma

canonical_form

normalized_form

primary_reading

writing_system
```

---

### lemma

The canonical lookup form.

This is the primary access key of the lexical system.

Example

```text
nfr
```

---

### canonical_form

Official normalized representation.

May differ from historical spellings.

---

### normalized_form

Machine-normalized form.

Used for indexing.

Searching.

Comparison.

---

### primary_reading

Preferred scholarly reading.

---

### writing_system

Hieroglyphic

Hieratic

Demotic

Coptic

Future writing systems

Referenced through Meta Cards.

---

# Academic Notes

Unlike traditional dictionaries, the Lexical Card is **not** a dictionary entry.

It is a **linguistic knowledge node**.

The dictionary is merely one way of displaying it.

The website is another.

The knowledge graph is another.

The AI model is another.

This separation between **knowledge** and **representation** is what allows Hypatia to become simultaneously:

* a digital lexicon,
* a scholarly corpus,
* a semantic graph,
* and a long-term training foundation for future language models.

## ✦ تعليق أخير

أعتقد أن تغيير الاسم من **Dictionary Card** إلى **Lexical Card** سيكون من أفضل القرارات المعمارية في المشروع. فهو يحرر البطاقة من كونها "مدخل قاموس" ويجعلها تمثل ما هي عليه فعلًا: **العقدة المركزية لكل المعرفة اللغوية** داخل هيباتيا. بل وأقترح أن يسمى المجلد أيضًا:

```text
repository/
└── lexicon/
```

بدلًا من `dictionary/`، لأن **Lexicon** هو المصطلح الأكاديمي المستخدم لوصف البنية المعجمية الكاملة للغة، وهو يعكس طموح المشروع بدقة أكبر.
