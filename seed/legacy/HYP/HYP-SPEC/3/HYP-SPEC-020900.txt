وهذه الوثيقة، في رأيي، هى **أهم وثيقة معمارية فى مشروع هيباتيا كله**.

وليس لأن الـ Resolver يربط الملفات فقط...

بل لأنه هو **العقل**.

إذا كانت:

* Meta هى الهوية.
* Lexical هى اللغة.

فإن **Resolver هو القشرة المخية (Cerebral Cortex)**.

لقد راجعت كل ما ناقشناه خلال الأشهر الماضية، وكل ما بنيناه من طبقات، وأعتقد أن الوقت قد حان لكتابة الـ Resolver كما سيعيش لعشرات السنين، وليس كما يعمل اليوم فقط.

# Purpose

The Resolver is the central knowledge orchestration engine of the Hypatia Repository.

Its responsibility is **not** to store knowledge.

Its responsibility is to understand how knowledge objects are connected.

Every intelligent operation inside Hypatia passes through the Resolver Layer.

---

# Philosophy

Knowledge Objects remain independent.

Resolvers create understanding.

Resolvers never own knowledge.

Resolvers only discover it.

---

# Scientific Definition

A Resolver is an independent engine responsible for discovering, validating, enriching, and navigating relationships between Knowledge Objects.

Resolvers transform isolated repositories into an integrated knowledge system.

---

# Architectural Position

```
User

↓

API

↓

Resolver Layer

↓

Knowledge Objects

↓

Repository
```

The Resolver Layer never bypasses the Repository.

It interprets it.

---

# Core Principles

The Resolver Layer follows five immutable principles.

## Principle 1

Knowledge Objects never know each other directly.

They reference only IDs.

---

## Principle 2

Resolvers construct relationships dynamically.

---

## Principle 3

Resolvers are stateless.

They never permanently modify Knowledge Objects.

---

## Principle 4

Resolvers may cooperate.

One Resolver may invoke another.

---

## Principle 5

Resolvers never duplicate repository data.

# Founder Statement

The Repository preserves knowledge.

The Resolver understands knowledge.

By separating storage from reasoning, Hypatia ensures that its scientific archive remains immutable while its intelligence continues to evolve.

Every future algorithm, every future AI model, every future reasoning engine will communicate with the Repository through the Resolver Layer, making the Resolver the living interpreter of Hypatia's accumulated knowledge.

---

# Academic Notes

Unlike conventional software where business logic is distributed across applications, Hypatia centralizes all semantic reasoning inside the Resolver Layer.

This separation creates three permanent advantages:

* Repository stability.
* Independent evolution of reasoning algorithms.
* Complete explainability of every decision.

The Resolver Layer therefore becomes the universal interface through which humans, software, and future AI systems interact with the Hypatia Knowledge Repository.
