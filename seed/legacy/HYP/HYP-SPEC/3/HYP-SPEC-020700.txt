# Purpose

The Relation Card represents a semantic relationship between two or more Knowledge Objects.

Relations transform isolated knowledge into an interconnected knowledge network.

Every edge in the Hypatia Knowledge Graph is represented by a Relation Card.

---

# Philosophy

Knowledge does not exist in isolation.

Everything knows something.

Everything belongs somewhere.

Everything influences something else.

A relation is therefore a Knowledge Object.

Not merely a database edge.

---

# Definition

A Relation Card describes one scientific relationship connecting two or more Knowledge Objects.

The relation itself possesses identity, provenance, confidence, history, and scientific meaning.

---

# Academic Notes

Unlike conventional graph databases where relationships are lightweight edges, Hypatia models every relation as an independent **Knowledge Object**.

This enables

* provenance,
* revision history,
* confidence scoring,
* contextual validity,
* competing interpretations,
* and scientific review.

Consequently, the graph itself becomes an evolving scientific artifact rather than a static network.

## **Relation Pattern**

وليس Relation فقط.

أى أن النظام لا يحفظ العلاقة المفردة فقط، بل يحفظ **النمط المتكرر للعلاقات**.

مثال:

```
King

↓

Builds

↓

Temple
```

هذا ليس Relation واحدة.

هذا Pattern.

ثم يأتى بعد ذلك:

```
Ramesses II

↓

Builds

↓

Abu Simbel
```

و

```
Amenhotep III

↓

Builds

↓

Luxor Temple
```

كلاهما يرثان من نفس **Relation Pattern**.
