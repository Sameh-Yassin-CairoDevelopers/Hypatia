# Purpose

The Inference Dataset Builder generates optimized knowledge packages used during inference by websites, APIs, search engines, AI assistants, and future reasoning systems.

Unlike Training Datasets, Inference Datasets are designed for fast retrieval rather than machine learning.

---

# Philosophy

Training teaches.

Inference answers.

The Repository remains the scientific authority.

Inference datasets are optimized views of Repository knowledge.

---

# Responsibilities

The Inference Dataset Builder is responsible for

* Selecting searchable knowledge.
* Building optimized lookup structures.
* Preparing semantic indexes.
* Exporting lightweight datasets.
* Preserving provenance.
* Synchronizing with Repository revisions.

It never modifies Repository Objects.

# Founder Statement

Scientific repositories are designed for preservation.

Applications are designed for interaction.

The Inference Dataset Builder bridges these two worlds.

It transforms the permanent scientific archive of Hypatia into efficient knowledge packages capable of powering dictionaries, research tools, intelligent assistants, APIs, and future reasoning systems without compromising the integrity of the Repository.

Every answer ultimately originates from the Repository, but every interaction is optimized through the Inference Layer.

---

# Academic Notes

The separation between Training Datasets and Inference Datasets is a foundational architectural decision.

Training datasets exist to optimize learning.

Inference datasets exist to optimize retrieval and interaction.

By maintaining these two independent products, Hypatia supports reproducible AI research, scalable deployment, and future Retrieval-Augmented Generation architectures while preserving a single authoritative scientific knowledge base.
