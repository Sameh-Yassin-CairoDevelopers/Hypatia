# Purpose

This specification defines the philosophy and architecture governing vector representations inside the Hypatia ecosystem.

Unlike conventional embedding systems that represent isolated words or tokens, Hypatia embeddings represent structured scientific knowledge derived from the Repository.

Embeddings are therefore semantic projections of Repository Objects rather than statistical representations of text alone.

---

# Philosophy

Words do not possess meaning.

Knowledge possesses meaning.

Embeddings should therefore represent knowledge rather than vocabulary.

---

# Fundamental Principle

Every embedding originates from a Repository Object.

No embedding exists without a traceable scientific origin.

---

# Academic Notes

The Hypatia Embedding Strategy adopts a **Knowledge-Centric Semantic Representation (KCSR)** architecture.

Rather than learning embeddings exclusively from token co-occurrence, vector representations are enriched using canonical lexical identity, grammatical metadata, named entities, historical context, and Knowledge Graph topology.

This produces explainable, traceable, and semantically grounded representations capable of supporting symbolic reasoning, Retrieval-Augmented Generation, graph-based learning, and future multimodal AI systems without compromising the scientific authority of the Repository.
